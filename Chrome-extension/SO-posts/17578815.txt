<p>This is a very simplified example, but yes. It is possible to have your results be displayed in a form.</p>

<p>What you need to do is create a new form (I used a JFrame Form).
From there, I added in a JTextPane to the form.</p>

<p>That should be all that you need on your form, just to display results.</p>

<p>After that, you will just need to set the textPane's text to your results and run the program.</p>

<p>This is what my code looks like.</p>
