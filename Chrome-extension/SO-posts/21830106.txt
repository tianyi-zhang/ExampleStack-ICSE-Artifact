<p><code>FlowLayout</code> will automatically "wrap" components whose width extends past the boundary of the container they're in. So, to accomplish <em>what I think</em> you're trying to accomplish, all you need to do is set the <code>FlowLayout</code> like you've done, and resize the <code>JTextField</code>s to the appropriate width.</p>
